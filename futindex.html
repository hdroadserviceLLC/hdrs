Who are we

HDRS is a leader in the truck tire service industry, providing top-notch roadside assistance to keep commercial vehicles running smoothly. Our team of skilled and experienced technicians are available 24/7 to provide emergency tire service for a wide range of vehicles including long haul, regional, P&D, mixed service, single tandem axel vehicles, RV, coach buses, and dump trucks (tires only).

We understand the importance of getting you back on the road quickly and safely, and our technicians are equipped with the latest tools and technology to get the job done efficiently and effectively. We pride ourselves on our quick response times and our commitment to providing excellent customer service.

Our goal is to minimize any downtime for your vehicle and minimize any inconvenience to you. We are here to help you keep your business running. With our team of experts and our commitment to providing top-notch service, you can trust that HDRS will keep your vehicles moving forward.
